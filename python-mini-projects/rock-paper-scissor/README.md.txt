# Rock Paper Scissor Game

Welcome to the **Rock Paper Scissor** game! Play with friends or against the computer. This game allows you to enjoy a classic strategy game with different modes.

## Features:
- **Single Player Mode**: Play against the computer.
- **Multi-Player Mode**: Play with a friend.
- Track your score across rounds.

## How to Play:
1. Choose between **Single Player** or **Multi-Player** mode.
2. Make your choice from "Rock", "Paper", or "Scissor".
3. The first to score wins the game.

## How to Run:
1.Download and run the script `main.py` to start the game.

Enjoy and may the best player win!
